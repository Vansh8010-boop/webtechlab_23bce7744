About VIT-AP

Vellore Institute of Technology, Amaravati (VIT-AP) University is a private university located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is committed to providing world-class education and research opportunities to its students.

Academics

VIT-AP offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across disciplines like Engineering, Sciences, and Management. The curriculum is designed to be flexible, allowing students to choose subjects that align with their interests and career goals.

Campus Life

The campus provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern classrooms, laboratories, a central library, and sports facilities. We encourage a vibrant campus life with numerous student clubs and technical societies.
